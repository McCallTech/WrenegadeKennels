    About Us

    Wrenegade Kennels is an established and well regarded German Shorthair Pointer breeder in Iowa. Breeding since 1970 and winning National Amateur Championship & Open in 1972, we have been involved in the Eastern Iowa German Shorthair Club for many years. Having judged at all of the national championships over the years and winning with our dogs, we've seen a collection of the best shorthairs in the history of the breed, and our own bloodlines include multiple FC and NFC dogs.

    After decades of highly selective breeding Wrenegade is producing excellent companions and top performers. We produce national caliber dogs with incredible natural abilities in every litter. Careful consideration is given to each breeding to make sure that the sire and dam are complimentary and the pups are well matched to the personalities and needs of our clients. References are available and the birds in the bag speak for themselves.
